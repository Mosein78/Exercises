Why Frontend Bootcamp ?

Our website is specifically designed to teach front-end skills so that anyone, from beginners to advanced enthusiasts, can become a professional front-end developer using a variety of project-based courses. In this BootCamp, our main focus is on teaching the basics of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Vue. All courses are designed in a step-by-step manner and include real projects and practical exercises that directly apply the skills learned. Also, our team of trainers and experts will accompany you throughout the course and guide you to improve by providing constructive feedback at every stage of learning.

FeaturesBootCamp

Careful and systematic planning

The duration of this bootcamp is 7 months and it is planned for all days of these 7 months so that you can have the highest learning in the shortest time.

Updates and Updates

All training and courses provided to you in this bootcamp are fully updated and based on the latest updates of each technology.

Support and mentoring

Throughout the learning process, a full support and mentoring team, along with a professor, is by your side to resolve any challenges and problems as quickly as possible.

Follow-up and continuation

Your smallest omission or evasion will be followed up by our team to determine and resolve the cause and provide ongoing training.

Project-oriented

In addition to the exercises you do during training, you will also be given a series of separate projects during the bootcamp that you must complete within a specific time frame.

Introduction to career paths

At the end of the bootcamp, you will have the opportunity to be hired on our team or complete an internship.

Interaction and networking

In this boot camp, you will be trained based on what the labor market needs so that you will not have any shortages in the future.

Online workshops and troubleshooting

In addition to the training we provide, we will also be holding a series of online workshops to implement and troubleshoot various projects as a group and discuss your problems and questions.
